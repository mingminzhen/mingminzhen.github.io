About

I'm a PH.D. student in department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology from 2016 to now, under the supervision of Prof. Long Quan. I received my Master degree in Computer Science from the Peking University in 2016 and Bachelor degree from NanKai University in 2013. My research interests are machine learning, semantic segmentation, saliency detection and their applications to computer vision.
